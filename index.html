Sweet Pineapple

Bright Roast Edition

Our Coffee

Experience the unique and refreshing taste of Pineapple and Melon tasted coffee.

Sweet pineapple is one of the product line in heritage coffee bright roast edition line up. It's offer a uniqe sensation of coffee that Malang has to offer. With its pineapple acidity and melon/caramel like sweetness, sweet pineapple could gives you a refreshing sensation to start your day.

Coffee source

Kebun Kopi Budug Asu

Processing Method

Sweet Pineapple is a mix variety Arjuna coffee (mostly komasti) which is processed using modified anaerobic natural process with addition of lactic fermentation process. This process enabled the green bean to develop various flavor precursor which is transformed into a refreshing flavor compound during roasting process
